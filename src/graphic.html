Cases by Immigration Court

The four immigration courts that have hearing locations dedicated to “Remain in Mexico” cases used to receive far fewer cases each month. That all changed this year after the “Remain in Mexico” policy took effect. While the number of other cases stays roughly steady, the number of “Remain in Mexico” cases drove each city’s caseload through the roof.
